CASE 001

The First Symbol

Act I - The Pattern

Background Story

A body was discovered in an abandoned warehouse on the outskirts of the city. The victim, identified as Marcus Webb, a former financial analyst, was found with a cryptic symbol carved into his chest. The symbol matches no known gang markings or religious symbols. Local police are baffled, but they've noticed this isn't the first such incident.

Your investigation begins here. The database contains witness statements, forensic reports, financial records, and surveillance data. Your task is to query the evidence, find connections, and uncover the truth behind this mysterious symbol.

Known Facts

  • Victim found at 3:47 AM in abandoned warehouse
  • Cryptic symbol carved into chest - unknown origin
  • No forced entry - killer had access or was let in
  • Victim was working on sensitive financial analysis
  • Similar symbols found at two other recent crime scenes

Investigation Objectives

1
Query the witnesses table to identify all individuals who saw the victim in the 24 hours before death
2
Analyze financial records to find unusual transactions linked to the victim
3
Cross-reference surveillance data with witness statements
4
Identify the meaning and origin of the cryptic symbol
5
Determine the killer and their motive
