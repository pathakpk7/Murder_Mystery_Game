Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Project Vritra

Do I need to know SQL to play?

No prior SQL knowledge is required. Project Vritra is designed to teach you SQL through investigation. Each case introduces progressively more complex SQL concepts, starting with basic SELECT queries and advancing to JOINs, aggregations, and subqueries. The Detective Academy (How to Play section) provides a comprehensive 7-step onboarding to get you started.

How are clues unlocked?

Clues are unlocked by completing objectives within each case. Each objective teaches a specific SQL concept and, when solved correctly, reveals new evidence or information about the case. The clues you unlock are stored in your Evidence Locker and can be referenced throughout the investigation. Some clues may also unlock new cases or story revelations.

Can I replay cases?

Yes, you can replay any case at any time. Replaying allows you to try different approaches, discover alternative solutions, or simply enjoy the story again. Your best scores and completion times are tracked separately, so you can challenge yourself to improve your performance on previously solved cases.

Are the cases connected?

Absolutely. Project Vritra tells a complete story across 18 cases organized into four acts. Each case builds on the previous ones, revealing new layers of the conspiracy. Clues unlocked in earlier cases may become crucial in later investigations. The story is designed to be experienced in order, but you can jump to any unlocked case if you prefer.

How long does it take to complete?

The total playtime varies depending on your SQL proficiency and how thoroughly you investigate. On average, each case takes 30-90 minutes to complete. With 18 cases, the full story experience typically takes 15-25 hours. However, you can play at your own pace—there's no time limit, and you can return to cases anytime.

Does the story have multiple endings?

Project Vritra has a single canonical ending that reveals the full truth behind the conspiracy. However, the final case presents you with a meaningful choice that affects how the story concludes thematically. While the core truth remains the same, your decision shapes the narrative's final message and your character's journey.

What happens if I use hints?

Hints are available for each objective, but they cost detective points. Using a hint will deduct 10 points from your score for that case. Hints provide guidance on the SQL approach without giving away the exact solution. Use them strategically—sometimes a small nudge is all you need to get back on track.

Can I adjust the difficulty?

Each case has a fixed difficulty rating (Easy, Medium, or Hard) based on the SQL concepts required. However, the progression is designed to be accessible—easier cases in Act I teach foundational skills that prepare you for more complex challenges in later acts. If you're struggling, the Detective Academy and hint system are there to help you learn and progress.

Is my progress saved?

Yes, your progress is automatically saved locally in your browser. This includes completed cases, unlocked clues, scores, and your current position in the story. As long as you use the same browser, your progress will be preserved between sessions. Note that clearing your browser data will reset your progress.

What are the technical requirements?

Project Vritra runs entirely in your web browser—no installation required. You need a modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge) and an internet connection to access the game. The game is optimized for both desktop and tablet devices. For the best experience, we recommend using a desktop browser with a screen resolution of at least 1024x768.
