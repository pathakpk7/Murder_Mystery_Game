THE STORY

A conspiracy that spans decades. A pattern hidden in plain sight. One detective determined to uncover the truth.

I

What Is Project Vritra

Project Vritra is not just a series of murders. It is a carefully orchestrated conspiracy that has been operating in the shadows for over two decades. The name comes from Hindu mythology—Vritra, the serpent of drought who hoarded water and brought suffering to the world. In our story, Vritra represents those who hoard truth, manipulate information, and control narratives to serve their own ends.

The project began as a covert operation within a powerful financial conglomerate. Its stated purpose was to identify and neutralize threats to corporate interests. But over time, it evolved into something far more sinister—a system for predicting human behavior, manipulating markets, and eliminating anyone who discovered too much.

"The truth is not hidden. It is buried under layers of carefully constructed lies. To find it, you must be willing to dig through the debris of deception."

You play as Prasoon Pathak, a detective who stumbled upon the first thread of this conspiracy. What began as a routine investigation into a single murder has become a journey into the heart of a system that controls everything from financial markets to personal destinies.

II

Who Is Prasoon Pathak

Prasoon Pathak is not your typical detective. A former data analyst turned private investigator, he approaches every case with the analytical precision of someone who spent years finding patterns in chaos. His background in financial forensics gives him a unique advantage—he sees connections that others miss, understands the language of money and data that most criminals use to hide their tracks.

But Prasoon is more than his skills. He is driven by a personal tragedy that haunts him still. Five years ago, his sister disappeared under mysterious circumstances. The police called it a voluntary disappearance. Prasoon knew better. He has been searching for her ever since, and his investigation into Project Vritra may finally lead him to answers about what really happened to her.

Data Analyst

Former financial forensics expert with an uncanny ability to find patterns in complex datasets.

Private Investigator

Now uses his analytical skills to solve cases that traditional investigators cannot crack.

Driven by Loss

Searching for his missing sister, her disappearance is the fire that fuels his determination.

IV

The Hidden Organization

Behind Project Vritra lies an organization that has operated in complete secrecy for decades. Known as the "Ninth Mandala" to its inner circle, it controls vast resources through a network of shell companies, offshore accounts, and compromised officials. The organization is led by "The Seven Shadows"—a secret leadership group that has manipulated events from the shadows for years.

The Ninth Mandala's power comes from its ability to predict and manipulate human behavior on a massive scale. Using advanced data analytics, psychological profiling, and financial leverage, they can influence elections, crash markets, and eliminate threats before they materialize. They are the puppet masters of the modern world, and until now, they have been invisible.

"The greatest trick the devil ever pulled was convincing the world he didn't exist. The Ninth Mandala has improved on that trick—they convinced the world that even the devil is just a conspiracy theory."

But every system has weaknesses. Every organization has vulnerabilities. And every conspiracy leaves traces. Your investigation will follow those traces, piece by piece, until the entire structure is exposed.

V

The Four Acts

The story of Project Vritra unfolds across four acts, each revealing a deeper layer of the conspiracy. Each act contains multiple cases, and each case brings you closer to the truth.

I

The Pattern

Strange murders with cryptic symbols. A pattern emerges from the chaos. The investigation begins.

II

The Conspiracy

Financial networks. Vanishing witnesses. The pattern has architects. The conspiracy reveals itself.

III

The System

Predictive systems. Fabricated evidence. Reality itself is manipulated. The system is exposed.

IV

The Architect

The final truth. The architect revealed. The cycle completes. The conspiracy ends—or begins anew.
